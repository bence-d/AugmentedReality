Usage

Health care

In Health care Augmented Reality can be used to assist when performing a complex operation and by showing additional Information.
For example, work steps or x-ray. Besides, it can support the planning and practice of surgical procedures or to teach students new things more effectively.

Tourism

In Tourism Augmented Reality can be used to show the name of streets and sights.
It can also provide more information for a certain location. The information can be provided by previous visitors.
It can also show simulations of historical events, places, or objects.

Navigation

When driving with a car Augmentented Reality can display the information on your windshield.
Then it can indicate direction of the route and the distance.
It can also be used to display weather, terrain, and road conditions.

Military

For the military Augmented Reality can help to locate people or points of interest.
It can also help to navigate better by showing airports, railroads, and street names.
Furthermore, it can warn you about potential dangerous.

Translation

Translators can use Augmented Reality to get the text from the camera and replace it with the translated text on the screen.

Industry

Augmented reality for industrial purposes can be used to support the manufacturing of the product.
It should help the workers by showing worksteps and reducing the amout of decicions the have to make.
