Smart Glasses

Augmented Reality Glasses are aso called Smart glasses but what they do? Smart Glasses capture the environment, by scanning them using a camera. They identify points where the Ar can overlay an 3D Object. It can find the positioning using either a marker or a tracker like for example a infrared, a laser, a sensor or an GPS.

Simply you can change the positioning of your Objects and directly see what the difference would be and dont need to place those Objects in real and change them afterwards. So with those Smart Glasses you can change and see the added Objects.

AR in Games

Augmented reality gaming is the integration of simpel game visual and audio content.

The first hit with an AR game was in 2016 with Pokemon Go, it is a very simple Game but with the Ar Objects and only an added sound. After 2016 Ar games became more populare. But they dont need to be that simple, they could also be very advanced.

Some games may actually build an enviroment by using the surroundings from the user. There could be a advanced Game where you can destroy buildings that are arround you and the Ar makes it look realistic. Like that it would be possible to interact with the Real World and the extended World from the Ar System.

VR Games

Virtual Reality games are very different then the AR versions. While in AR the Real World gets extdended and featured elements apear on your Phone/Glasses. In Virtual Reality an new World is created, most of the time not even the same like the Real World, where you interact with Virtual Objects that can be different from Real Life or exactly the same.

There could also be a Virtual World that look simulare like the Real World.
